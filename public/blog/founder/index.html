The vision powering IDEM springs from a new generation of technical builders. At the helm of this ecosystem is Ngawang Tshogyal Phuentshok, a forward-thinking student leader from The Royal Academy in Paro, Pangbisa (Batch of 2024). Merging structural software architecture with civic intentionality, Ngawang handles the core programmatic scaling of the ecosystem alongside a dedicated team of co-builders.

What is IDEM?

IDEM is a forward-thinking social media platform driven by a core vision: to provide users with equal and rightful access to information across the internet. We believe in helping our users discover their inner "Ter"—meaning the inner treasure or unique talent that resides within everyone.

Beyond just connecting people, IDEM is a collaborative space where users can share knowledge, voice their opinions, host events, and work together. The platform also empowers users by providing them with a comprehensive digital profile and detailed reports on their digital health.

The Rapid Ascent of EduLink

As the proud parent organization of EduLink, IDEM serves to unify learning communities. Under Ngawang’s foundational development and full-stack execution, EduLink has experienced remarkable initial growth. In just one single month since its formal launch, the application has successfully scaled to approximately 350 active users, indicating a strong early demand for unified academic utility.

Ngawang remains heavily hands-on with the implementation roadmap, engineering key updates and collaborating continuously with fellow team members to iterate on early user feedback.

The IDEM Ecosystem

To support our ambitious vision, IDEM is powered by a network of specialized internal organizations managed under Ngawang's broader ecosystem mapping:

IRC (Idem Research Center)

As the official research arm of IDEM, the IRC operates at the intersection of human psychology and technology. Its primary mission is to conduct in-depth research to continuously improve EduLink and other IDEM projects.

Note on Privacy: We take user privacy incredibly seriously. Research conducted at the IRC is highly confidential and closely supervised. Because our studies often involve sensitive data from user interviews and testing, this information is strictly protected and is never shared with third-party organizations.

IEPD (Idem Engineering and Product Design)

IEPD is the creative and technical engine of our ecosystem. This division is dedicated entirely to the engineering, product development, and user-centric design that brings IDEM's platforms to life. It is here that Ngawang concentrates his coding frameworks and system deployment goals.

IMF (Idem Marketing and Foundation)

Focused on our outreach, brand growth, and foundational initiatives across regional borders.

Newton & Sherig

Key organizational pillars working within the IDEM network to support our broader educational and technological goals.
