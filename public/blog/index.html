The Launch of EduLink at The Royal Academy

What began as a passionate side project has rapidly evolved into something much larger. Today, EduLink is being actively developed and refined by our dedicated core team. We are currently implementing comprehensive upgrades to the platform's security infrastructure, ensuring a completely safe digital environment for all users.

Our primary mission is to empower students. EduLink provides a secure space designed not just for communication, but for growth—giving students direct access to entrepreneurial resources and placing a specialized focus on the tourism sector.

The Launch Event

On Saturday, April 4th, we proudly introduced EduLink to the student body at The Royal Academy. Members of the EduLink development team led a comprehensive public presentation that detailed the platform's vision and capabilities. We are thrilled to report that the presentation was a massive success, met with overwhelming enthusiasm from the student community.

Image courtesy of EduLink

Campus Integration

Following the successful announcement, EduLink took another major step forward: the platform was officially integrated directly into The Royal Academy's main school website. This integration bridges our application with the broader academic infrastructure, making access seamless for all students and faculty.

Image courtesy of The Royal Academy

Community Traction

The response following the presentation has been nothing short of incredible. Spurred by the launch, our community has already grown to roughly 250 active users. More impressively, these users have generated almost 120 posts, leading to massive interaction and collaborative energy flowing between the students on the platform.

Image courtesy of EduLink

Looking Ahead

The momentum continues to build. Next week, the EduLink team will be hosting a dedicated presentation tailored specifically for the academy's teachers, gathering their feedback and exploring new ways to support their curriculum.

On the technical front, we are currently undertaking a major refactoring of our framework, transitioning entirely to TypeScript to ensure better scalability and maintainability. Furthermore, our upcoming content and feature roadmap will place a special thematic focus on Earth Day initiatives.

Image courtesy of EduLink

As our scope expands, so does our team. We are incredibly excited to welcome Rigzin and Pema Thinley to EduLink. They will be spearheading the highly anticipated "Newton's" development phase. The future is bright, and we are just getting started.
